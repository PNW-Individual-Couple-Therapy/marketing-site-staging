Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the website (Website) of Pacific Northwest Individual and Couple Therapy, PLLC (Practice) and governs collection, usage, and disclosure of your information through the Website. Please take a moment to review this Privacy Policy.

Use & Collection of Personal Information

Any personally identifiable information (personal information), such as name, email address, physical address, and/or telephone number, submitted by Website users may be used by Practice for reasonable business purposes. Practice may use your personal information to communicate with you.

Sharing of Information

Any information submitted by you through the Website is for the exclusive use of Practice and any contracted service providers for the purpose of the operation of the Website. Practice does not sell, trade, or rent any personal information. Practice may save personal information to comply with federal and state policies, but it does not disclose this information to third parties or government agencies unless state or federal law requires us to do so, such disclosure is necessary to provide the Website to you, or exigent circumstances require Practice to protect the safety of its users or the public.

Security of Personal Information

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, Practice has established appropriate and reasonable security procedures (physical, electronic, and administrative) to safeguard and secure the information Practice collects.

However, please remember that the Internet is not a 100% secure environment, and therefore Practice cannot and does not guarantee that information you provide through the Practice website may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.

Automatic Data Collection Technologies

Like many websites, we may use automatic data collection technologies to enhance the user experience and to collect helpful information about visitors and visits to our website. We also may use these technologies to collect information about your online activities over time and across third-party websites or other online services. The information we collect automatically is not personal information and may include IP addresses, internet tags and navigational data (server log files), which may be used by Practice or shared with third parties. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

  • Cookies (or browser cookies). Cookies are pieces of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Cookies are used to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services.
  • Flash Cookies. Certain features of our Website may use local stored objects (or Flash cookies) to collect and store information about your preferences and navigation to, from, and on our Website. Flash cookies are not managed by the same browser settings as are used for browser cookies
  • Web Beacons. Pages of the Website and our e-mails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs).

Practice will not share any information collected through cookies with any third-party except to the extent they are performing services on behalf of Practice.

Third-Party Websites

Practice may include links to third-party websites on the Website. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. Practice therefore has no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked websites. Practice encourages you to read the privacy policies of each of these third-party websites.

Opt-Out Procedures

Users may opt-out of receiving future e-mails or mailings from Practice. To remove your identifiable information from our database or to no longer receive communications from Practice, please contact Practice at the address or telephone number provided below.

Parents and Guardians.

Practices Website does not target and is not intended to attract children under the age of 183. Practice does not solicit personally identifiable information from minors through our Website.

Acceptance of These Terms

Your usage of this Website signifies your acceptance of the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use this website.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right, at any time, to modify this Privacy Policy. If we make revisions that change the way we collect, use, or share personal information, we will post those changes in this Privacy Policy. You should review this Privacy Policy periodically so that you keep up to date on our most current policies and practices.
